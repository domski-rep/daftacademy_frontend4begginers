STRAIGHT FROM LUBLIN

6BM is a young band which has it's origins in the city of Lublin, playing music described as electrorock. Band members aim to convey emotions, reflexion but also hope.

They are inspired by varuous genres, like: alternative rock, nu-metal, synthewave or even drum'n'bass. As for now they released their debut EP - TRINITY and they started working on their first full length album.
